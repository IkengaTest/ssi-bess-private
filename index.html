BESS Valuation Platform

Substation-Level Investment Intelligence for Battery Energy Storage

A proprietary valuation engine scoring 4,293 Italian distribution substations for BESS deployment suitability — combining grid resilience analytics, socio-economic consequence modelling, and real options pricing into a single investment-grade metric.

Built on the SSI Index v4.0 — the first composite score fusing grid engineering with societal exposure — the platform translates resilience signals into actionable investment returns.

From Risk to Return

The SSI identifies where the grid is most stressed. The BESS platform converts that stress into quantified investment opportunity — NPV, IRR, and payback for two standardised configurations at every substation.

Dual Configuration Analysis

Every substation is evaluated under Config A (4.9 MW / 19.6 MWh, €3.5M CAPEX) and Config B (9.9 MW / 39.6 MWh, €7.0M CAPEX), enabling direct comparison across investment scales.
