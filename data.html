Processing

Data Pipeline

From raw source to investment metric — the platform processes data through a six-stage pipeline.

1

Ingest

95 variables from 30 verified public data sources — E-Distribuzione, ARERA, ISTAT, Copernicus CDS, OSM, GSE, ENTSO-E, and 23 others. Zero proprietary SCADA dependencies.

2

Normalise

Four normalisation methods: Method A (robust fleet percentile P5/P95), Method B (standard percentile), Method C (bounded rescaling), and Method D (categorical mapping).

3

Score

6-component weighted sum produces R_base. Five multiplicative modifiers (R2–R7) adjust for geographic, demographic, topological, seismic, and cyber context.

4

Simulate

10,000 Monte Carlo iterations per substation using a 20×20 Gaussian copula correlation matrix. Produces median, P5, P95, and confidence intervals.

5

Value

Three-pillar value stack (V_DNO + V_Community + V_RO) computed for dual BESS configurations. NPV, IRR, and payback derived at WACC 5.2% over 25-year horizon.

6

Rank

Fleet-wide ranking by risk-adjusted NPV. Investment priority assigned. Configuration recommendation (A or B) based on highest NPV.
